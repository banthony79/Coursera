This is my receipe for slow cooked pork

  1. Take pork and put a bunch of salt and pepper on them.
    2. Put in slow cooker
  2. Dump two cans of mushroom soup in with pork
  3. Dump package of herb and garlic seasoning
    4. Turn on slow cooker for 8 hours on low
  4. Serve with rice and hot sauce goes well on too

    5. this is a speed test Ta Da!
